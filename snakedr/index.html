Instructions

Save the snake by eliminating all the letters from its body while scoring as many points as possible. Points are deducted for every letter used, and earned for every >4 letter word created.

Helpful tips

Use your pills by clicking on the snake to instantly eliminate a letter. Pills can only be used if there are more than 3 letters in the snake. You will earn more pills for every >5 letter word created.

You will get the same starting letters and draws each day. You re-attempt the day's puzzle to get a better score.
